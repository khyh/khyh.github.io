Decoding reCAPTCHA


(This web page was created on February 2014.)

Summary

This homepage demonstrates that reCAPTCHA can not tell computers from humans apart. I created a decoder and tested on Intel Xeon triple-core VPS server. In this experiment, it tried 175 times and passed 56 (32.0 %) of them. It is far from 'practical', but it can be said that reCAPTCHA is not as safe as it thought to be.

Since the decoder can be abused by spammers, I only publish results for now.
